Recruiter Dashboard

View all open job postings and active candidates
Drag-and-drop candidates between stages (Kanban pipeline view)
Bulk actions: move, reject, email, schedule interview

Job Management

Create, edit, and publish job descriptions
Attach screening questions and required skills
Multi-channel posting: LinkedIn, Indeed, company site

Candidate Profile

Unified candidate card: CV, contact info, skills, experience, notes
Resume parsing (PDF/DOCX → structured data)
AI-based quality scoring

Application Pipeline

Configurable pipeline stages: Applied → Screening → Interview → Offer → Hired
Automations: trigger emails or tasks when stage changes
Tagging: e.g. “Strong JS”, “Needs follow-up”, “Referred”
History and timeline of recruiter actions

Interview Scheduling

Integrate with Google Calendar / Outlook
Automatic time slot suggestions based on availability
Multi-round interview templates (HR, technical, final)
Feedback forms attached to events

Communication Center

Unified inbox (email + chat with candidates)
Automated email templates (“Application received”, “Rejected”, etc.)
WhatsApp / SMS integration for reminders
AI-suggested responses for candidate communication

Task & Collaboration

Assign tasks to team members (e.g. “Review CV by Monday”)
Shared notes and mentions (@John check this candidate)
Audit trail for compliance

Resume Intelligence

AI parsing with skill/entity extraction (NER)
AI CV Scoring: match % between job description & candidate resume
AI-based recommendation: “Top 5 matching candidates”
Suggest missing skills or red flags

Analytics & Reporting

Time-to-hire, source-of-hire, drop-off analysis
Funnel conversion: Applied → Interviewed → Hired
Recruiter performance metrics
Diversity & inclusion reporting
Export to Excel / Google Sheets
Candidate Portal: apply, track status, update CV

AI CV improvement suggestions
Personalized job recommendations
Interview preparation or mock interviews
Notifications (email, SMS, in-app)

Job boards (LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, VietnamWorks)
HRIS/Payroll systems (BambooHR, Workday)
Email & calendar sync
Slack / Teams notification integration

Access Control & Compliance
Role-based access: Admin, Recruiter, Hiring Manager
Audit logs of all actions
